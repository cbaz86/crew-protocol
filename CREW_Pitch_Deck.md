## SLIDE 3: THE SOLUTION

### CREW: Coordinated Robot Emergency Workforce

**HOW IT WORKS:**

**1. EMERGENCY BROADCAST**  
Fire command sends: "Need thermal imaging + debris clearing"

**2. ROBOTS DECIDE**  
Each robot evaluates: Can I help? Is it safe? Am I allowed?

**3. HUMAN COORDINATES**  
Dashboard shows available robots. Dispatcher assigns tasks.

**4. ROBOTS ASSIST**  
Thermal drone locates survivors. Ground bot clears path.

**KEY INSIGHT:** Robots volunteer. Humans decide. No central control.

**VISUAL:** Animated flowchart showing the 4 steps

**SPEAKER NOTES:**  
"CREW is opt-in, capability-based, and human-led. It's not Skynet‚Äîit's a coordination protocol, like how 911 dispatches ambulances from different hospitals."
